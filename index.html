WELCOME TO FUTURE TOOLS FOR YOUTH DATA LITERACIES WORKSHOP!

The Workshop is scheduled for July 13 & 15, 2021 and will be held online as part of the 2021 Connected Learning Summit. Our workshop aims to inspire the design of future tools for youth data literacies.

If you want to join fill out this short pre-registration form! To attend you will also need to register on the 2021 Connected Learning Summit website to access the conference platform. The workshop will take place in two sessions:

  • Session 1 (July 13th at 11 am PST): we will explore and evaluate a list of existing tools that help young people understand and work with data.
  • Session 2 (July 15th at 11 am PST): we will brainstorm and design new tools and features to help young people develop data literacies.
Participants can choose to attend one or both sessions. We welcome researchers, educators, and other practitioners who are interested in the topic of youth data literacies to attend our workshop!
