WELCOME TO THE IMAGINING FUTURE DESIGNS OF TOOLS FOR YOUTH DATA LITERACIES WORKSHOP!

The Imagining Future Designs of Tools For Youth Data Literacies workshop is scheduled on July 13th & 15th, 2021 and will be held online as part of the 2021 Connected Learning Summit.

If you are interested, please fill out this short pre-registration form by 9PM PST, July 10th! Pre-registration can help us integrate your expertise and expectation into the workshop experience. You also need to register on the 2021 Connected Learning Summit website to access the online conference platform.

Our workshop aims to inspire the design of future tools for youth data literacies. It will take place in two sessions:

  • Session 1 (July 13th at 11AM - 12PM PST): we will explore and evaluate a list of existing tools that help young people understand and work with data.
  • Session 2 (July 15th at 11AM - 12PM PST): we will brainstorm and design new tools and features to help young people develop data literacies.

Participants can choose to attend one or both sessions of the workshop. We welcome researchers, educators, and other practitioners who are interested in the topic of youth data literacies to attend our workshop!
