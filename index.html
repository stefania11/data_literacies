WELCOME TO THE WORKSHOP OF IMAGINING FUTURE DESIGNS OF TOOLS FOR YOUTH DATA LITERACIES!

The Workshop of Imagining Future Designs of Tools For Youth Data Literacies is scheduled on July 13 & 15, 2021 and will be held online as part of the 2021 Connected Learning Summit.

Our workshop aims to inspire the design of future tools for youth data literacies. It will take place in two parts:

  • Part 1 (July 13th at 11 am PST): we will explore and evaluate a list of existing tools that help young people understand and work with data.
  • Part 2 (July 15th at 11 am PST): we will brainstorm and design new tools and features to help young people develop data literacies.

Participants can choose to attend one or both parts of the workshop. We welcome researchers, educators, and other practitioners who are interested in the topic of youth data literacies to attend our workshop!

If you are interested, please first fill out this short pre-registration form! Pre-registration is due on July 10th at 9 pm PST and we will announce participant acceptance on July 11th. Once accepted, you need to register on the 2021 Connected Learning Summit website to access the conference platform.
